WHAT IS THIS SITE?

This site is a collection of information about my favorite military aircraft throughout the ages.
Each page consists of a picture of a plane, some information about the plane and something about it that I like/dont like/think is worth mentioning. You can find them in the "articles" tab, or an article in the "newest" tab to see recent additions.

Article catagorisation

The articles are catagorised into three nations, Ussr, Usa, and Europe. Yes, I know europe is not a nation, but different europian countries keep teaming up to make aircraft and to have each country seperately would get confusing fast.
