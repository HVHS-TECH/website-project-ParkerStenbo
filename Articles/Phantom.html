What is the Phantom?

The Phantom is a twin seat supersonic jet interceptor and fighter bomber that entered service with the US navy in 1961.
This aircraft ended up filling many roles throughout the US military and even saw overseas use in countries like britain.
Eventually it was replaced by aircraft like the f15, f16, f18, f14, and finally being retired from the united states military in 1996.
While it is no longer in use in the united states, some countries like Turkey still use this aircraft to this day and don't intend to stop for another few years.



My personal opinion

This aircraft gained many nicknames over it's time in service, but the one that has really stuck with me is "the flying brick."
It gained this nickname due to it's design philosiphy of "problem? make engine bigger." and I think the design reflects this.
While I have no experience flying any of these aircraft in real life, I've flown these aircraft in flight simulators like War Thunder and DCS world, and I believe it gets a bit too much hate.
Sure, planes like the mig21 and f5 demolish it in a sustained fight, planes like the mig25 and f104 that are significantly worse.
