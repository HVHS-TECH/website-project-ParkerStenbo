What is the strikemaster

The strikemaster is a jet aircraft used as a trainer by the Royal New Zealand Air Force.
The aircraft was made by BAC (british aircraft coorperation) as an armed version of the BAC jet provost mk 5.
While BAC intended this to be, and marketed it as, a light attack aircraft, most buyers just used it as a jet trainer. One of these buyers was New zealand.
