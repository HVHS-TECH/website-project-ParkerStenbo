What is the Berkut

The su47 Berkut is a soviet experemental aircraft designed as an "experemental supersonic technology demonstrator".
the most notable feature of this aircraft, and the one I will be talking about the most is the reverse swept wing.
the reverse swept wing has a few advantages, such as allowing an aircraft to maintane roll control at high angles of attacks, and asymetric stalls start at the wing root rather than the wing tip, reducing the amount of torque in an asymetric stall.
However, this form of design is more structurally complex, and high angle of attack manuvers put more stress on the wing than they do to more traditional wing designs.
Sadly the cost outweights the benifit and this consept stayed as an experimental one.



My personal opinion

I don't really have much to say other than it's super cool.
Despite appearing to be an early fourth gen fighter, it still looks extremely futuristic at first glance due to the unconventional design.
It looks just as elgant as other sukhoi aircraft, but the different parts of it don't quite fit together as well as they do on, say the su57 and su27 giving it a vibe similar to the f15 and f4, which makes me suprised that it first flew in the 1990s.
