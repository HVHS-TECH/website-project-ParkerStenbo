What is the fishbed

The mikoyan-gurevich mig-21 is a supersonic jet fighter and intercept aircraft that first flew in 16 of june 1955.
It was built as a continuation of the mig fighter line, the mig15, mig17, and mig19.
This aircraft, much like the phantom is still in service in smaller air forces despite being seven decades old.



My personal opinion

I think it shares a look with the F104 and F4, as if it was built to prioritize speed over manuverability and came out optimised for both. Nothing screams "I'M FAST" like a noze-cone in the intake.
I might even go as far as to say that it looks even more old-fashioned than the phantom, as it is quite literally an engine with a cockpit and wings strapped to it.
