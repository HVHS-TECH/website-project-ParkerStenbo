What is the Warthog?

The A-10 Thunderbolt II (typically reffered to as the "warthog") is a single-seat subsonic attack aricraft that has been in service since 1977.
The A-10 was designed to provide close air support to ground troops by attacking enemy ground forces.
It's most famous feature is it's gun. A 30mm GAU-8 avenger cannon with a fire rate of 3,900 rounds per minuite, It's especially well known how this aircraft was designed around the gun, built for the soul purpose of being practically a flying lazer beam that is capable of carrying guided weaponary as a bonus.



My personal opinion

The a10 is built like a brick. Even more so than the Phantom, it's just a gun with a box around it that has wings, a tail, and a couple of engines stuck outisde it. The design feels almost modular, like the engineers could change something with minimal difference to the rest of the aircraft, and I belive this gives it a charm.
